Advisory

Growth is enhanced by working collaboratively

To become successful in a competitive landscape is incredibly challenging and founders are frequently expected to be experts in all business, technical and market areas.

Part of the journey for start-ups and Fintechs is learning how to avoid mistakes and maximise efficiency by surrounding yourself with the right strategic advisors and support networks.

Finding advisors that fit in with your organisational culture and the specific challenges your business faces is difficult, at Ftechfolk we have created an exclusive advisory network to help connect you with strategic advisors, that complement you and your business. Advisors who have extensive, successful previous experience, who can help with the steep learning curve of being a first-time, venture-backed CEO.

It is very hard finding advisors who are genuinely committed to helping you, without help from professionals and many mistakes are made when there is very little time to learn.

We understand that no business is the same and every company may require a flexible network of advisors to call upon.

Getting to know you

It is critical that we get to know you and your business to ensure you get the most out of our network. By understanding your company, we are able to connect you with the right Advisor that will enable you to unlock growth and scale.

Our Advisors have demonstrable expertise and understanding of the Fintech industry, and the journey that Founders undertake when scaling a business. We carefully bring together those who will bring value to the specific businesses in our network and provide the essential framework to achieve success.
