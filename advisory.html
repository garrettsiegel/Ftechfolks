Advisory

Enhancing Growth through Collaboration

To become successful in a competitive, fast-evolving landscape is incredibly challenging and founders are frequently expected to be experts in all business, technical and market areas.

Part of the journey for start-ups and Fintechs is learning how to avoid mistakes and maximise efficiency. This can be accelerated by surrounding yourself with the right advisors and support networks.

At Ftechfolk we have created an exclusive advisory network to connect you with strategic advisors who complement you and your business.

Our advisors have extensive, successful previous experience. They have delivered successful projects from ‘idea’ stage to completion and can help with the steep learning curve of being a first-time, venture-backed CEO, as well as adding value to more established companies. A common mistake in recruiting advisors is to think that someone who’s been successful themselves can do the same for you, but not everyone who has been successful themselves makes a good advisor.

The right advisors fit in with your organisational culture and the specific challenges your business faces is difficult. They are genuinely committed to helping you, and will care about success as much as you do. Mistakes in this selection process can be extremely costly, especially when the pressure is on.

Ftechfolk understand that each business will have its own specific demands and has established a flexible network of friendly and highly capable advisors to call upon that can accommodate all of your needs.

Getting to know you

No two businesses are the same,
even in the same sector.

It is critical that we get to know you and your business to ensure you get the most out of our network. An advisory relationship that “should” work on paper often doesn’t. So much comes down to common values, complementary personalities and commitment levels.

By understanding you, your company and our individual advisors, we carefully assess the right match, connecting you with Advisors who will add value, enabling you to unlock growth and scale.

Our Advisors have demonstrable expertise and understanding of the Fintech industry, and the journey that Founders undertake when scaling a business. We carefully assess potential advisors, bringing together those who will work best with the specific businesses in our network, allowing them to enjoy a new challenge and new opportunities.
