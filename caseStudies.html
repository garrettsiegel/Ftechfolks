Case Studies

Ftechfolk locate and secure great people to fill critical and pivotal positions for our clients.
Our extensive knowledge of the Fintech market and outstanding networks of highly experienced folk ensures that we deliver every time!

We cover every aspect of Fintech projects from design/strategy and architecture through to compliance/regulatory and product or service development (and all stages in between).

Below are just a few examples of recently completed assignments.

Case Study 1

A leading Crypto bank based in London required a CISO following the sudden departure of their existing person due to illness. This was a highly critical position with severe consequences if not covered swiftly.

Ftechfolk found a suitable replacement with the necessary experience and the position was covered within one week.

Case Study 2

A new Fintech being established had engaged with an agency to provide a Compliance Lead for their new project. This was a highly critical position that required in depth knowledge of the environment and the ability to become the nominated person responsible for FCA license approval. The agency advised that the salary banding was 50% below that necessary in order to attract the right person.

FTechfolk located and secured an exceptional person within the original budget.

Case Study 3

A new Fintech required a highly experienced Head of Product in order to refine and drive forward their new offering to the market.

Ftechfolk located and secured an exceptional candidate with extensive experience within the Fintech environment and within the restricted budget allowed.
